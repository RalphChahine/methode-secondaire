Before choosing a tutor, I would clarify three things:

1. the exact grade and course;
2. the chapter or type of exercise causing trouble;
3. the date of the next test or exam.

We first match the tutor and confirm a realistic time. Then we recommend the right format: a **Targeted session** ($65 for 60 minutes) for one clear priority, a **Momentum block** (4 × 60 minutes for $250, one payment) to regain momentum, or a **Progress block** (10 × 60 minutes for $600, two $300 payments) for a difficulty that keeps returning.

For a block, credits are added only after the payment is verified. A weekly or biweekly time can be agreed with the tutor after matching; it is a scheduling preference, never a subscription or automatic renewal.

If helpful, send me the grade, subject and what is blocking progress, and I can suggest the most logical format.
