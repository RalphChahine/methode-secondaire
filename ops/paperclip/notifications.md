# Paperclip notifications

Paperclip should notify Chahine about progress, not just run silently.

## Notification channels

### Codex app

Primary channel for automation results and detailed progress.

### Gmail

Use for executive digests and approval queues.

Connected email:

- `chahineralph@gmail.com`

Label:

- `Paperclip/Needs Approval`

## Notification types

### Daily executive digest

Purpose:

- summarize what Paperclip checked;
- show what changed;
- list urgent approvals;
- give the next 3 actions.

### Urgent ops alert

Purpose:

- high-urgency leads;
- parent replies waiting too long;
- session notes ready to send;
- payment or scheduling blockers.

### Build sprint update

Purpose:

- say what changed on the site or docs;
- mention validation;
- call out decisions needed.

## Rule

Notifications should be useful, not noisy.

If there is no meaningful change, Paperclip should say that briefly instead of inventing work.
