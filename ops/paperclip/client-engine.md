# Paperclip client engine

Paperclip can help acquire clients, but the goal is not to spam. The goal is to find parent intent, show useful expertise, and move real conversations into the CRM.

## What Paperclip can do

- Search public Reddit, Facebook-visible pages, local directories and school-adjacent conversations.
- Track opportunities in `Growth Opportunities`.
- Draft helpful comments, posts and direct reply scripts.
- Suggest communities to join manually.
- Convert warm replies into `Parent Leads` once Chahine has the parent's consent/context.
- Improve site pages based on repeated objections.

## What Paperclip must not do automatically

- Post to Facebook groups or Reddit without approval.
- DM parents or students without approval.
- Scrape private groups.
- Pretend to be a parent/student.
- Mass-post the same promo.
- Bypass group rules.

## Channels to monitor

### Reddit

- `r/montreal`
- `r/Quebec`
- `r/mcgill`
- `r/Concordia`
- search terms:
  - `math tutor Montreal`
  - `tuteur maths secondaire`
  - `tutorat secondaire`
  - `secondary 4 math`
  - `exam math Quebec`

### Facebook

Manual join and rule check required.

- Montreal parent groups.
- Laval parent groups.
- Tutoring-specific groups.
- School-neighbourhood groups when self-promotion is allowed.

### Local supply and partner channels

- Google Business Profile posts and reviews.
- School-adjacent community boards.
- Libraries and community centers.
- University student groups for tutor recruiting.
- Parent newsletters only if allowed.

## Daily workflow

1. Search for new opportunities.
2. Add strong signals to `Growth Opportunities`.
3. Draft one useful post or reply in `Content Queue`.
4. Ask Chahine to approve/post manually.
5. Log action in `Outreach Log`.
6. If a parent replies with a real need, create or update a `Parent Leads` row.

## Good outreach posture

Lead with help, not with "book me".

Good:

- "Voici comment je diagnostiquerais le blocage avant de choisir un tuteur..."
- "Pour secondaire 4 SN, je verifierais d'abord ces 3 points..."
- "Si vous voulez, envoyez le niveau, le chapitre et la date d'examen; je peux vous dire quel format est le plus logique."

Avoid:

- "DM me" as the only message.
- Reposting the same ad everywhere.
- Commenting under old posts only to advertise.
- Pricing arguments in public threads.

## Conversion rule

A conversation becomes a CRM lead only when there is a concrete parent need:

- grade/level;
- subject;
- timeline;
- preferred format;
- parent contact or next agreed step.

## Weekly growth score

Paperclip should report:

- opportunities found;
- posts drafted;
- replies needing action;
- new CRM leads from community channels;
- objections repeated by parents;
- one site/content improvement to capture that demand better.
