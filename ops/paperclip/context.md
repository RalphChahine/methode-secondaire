# Paperclip context

## Core business

Methode Secondaire helps Quebec high-school families with math, science, homework support, exam prep and a 10-session progress block when continued support is useful. A weekly or biweekly cadence is agreed only after matching.

## Current strategic choice

The first parent/tutor portal version now exists.

Current portal scope:

- email-code login;
- parent dashboard;
- tutor dashboard;
- payment links;
- tutor session notes;
- parent follow-up requests.

Keep it portal-lite until the workflow proves it needs more.

Build and stabilize this before adding account complexity:

- CRM capture;
- tutor roster and availability;
- scheduled sessions;
- session notes;
- parent updates after sessions;
- weekly Paperclip reviews.

The full portal becomes a later product when repeated parent needs are clear.

## Current Paperclip loops

- Daily: find what needs action today.
- Client scout: find public demand and channel opportunities.
- Content queue: draft helpful posts/replies for approval.
- Weekly ops review: find bottlenecks and choose the next operational improvement.
- Weekly build sprint: implement one small, low-risk improvement.
- Monthly portal gate: decide whether the portal is still too early or ready for discovery.
