# VistaPrint Business Card

Files:

- `business-card-front.svg`
- `business-card-back.svg`
- `qr-website.svg`

Recommended upload mapping in VistaPrint:

1. Upload `business-card-front.svg` as the front.
2. Upload `business-card-back.svg` as the back.

These files were built to VistaPrint's current standard business card bleed size:

- Bleed: `3.61 x 2.11 in`
- Trim: `3.50 x 2.00 in`
- Safe area: `3.36 x 1.86 in`
- Pixel bleed size: `1083 x 633 px`

Official source:

- VistaPrint Business Card Size & Dimension Guide: https://www.vistaprint.com/hub/business-card-dimensions

Notes:

- The artwork already includes full bleed.
- The back design includes a QR code to `https://methode-secondaire.vercel.app/`.
- Keep using the SVG files directly for the cleanest upload.
- If VistaPrint warns about font substitution, convert the SVG to PDF before upload, or tell me and I can prepare a PDF-ready version next.

Recommended VistaPrint finish for this design:

- Best premium feel: `Soft Touch`
- Best thicker clean look: `Premium Plus Matte`
- Best artisanal alternative: `Cotton`
- Best shape choice for this specific layout: `Rounded corners`

Design direction used for this revision:

- Less visible copy and more negative space
- QR presented on its own, without the long URL underneath
- Strong phone-first hierarchy, with booking as the secondary action
- Minimal front, cleaner back, and fewer decorative blocks
